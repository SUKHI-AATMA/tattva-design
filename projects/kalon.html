  • Kalon

    Sector: Nutrition

    Services: Positioning, Messaging, Brand Identity, Design (UI/UX), Brand Communication, Product and packaging design.

    Kalon Agro is focused on developing novel fast nutrition products based on plant extracts. The brand offers traditional wisdom through unique product solutions which meet modern lifestyle's wellness needs. The mission is to make nature's goodness appealing to any age group with innovative flavours and product usability. Kalon has developed modern innovations for easy-to-handle product design, palatable across today's fast-paced consumption demands.

    We chose the route of bringing Indian heritage back into reckoning, by showcasing the brand's genuine traditional wellness knowledge in contemporary design & branding approach. Our goal was to highlight their subtlety in innovation with globally accepted design language, while retaining the Indian ethos.

  • Brand Identity

    The ancient wise men believed true wellness is harmony between body, mind and soul. In Indian shastras, Ida, Pingala and Sushumna represent the flow of energy within man. The symbol of rhythmically intertwining energy channels also finds its parallel in the still prevalent Greek mythological symbol: The Rod of Asclepius, the universal emblem of healing and medicine. We were inspired by the DNA as well as the serpent, believed to be the secret code to explore human evolution.

    The lotus called as Sahasrara, the Crown Chakra, is included, establishing Indian heritage of harmony and spiritual ascent. This also signals the herbal aspect of this incredible brand. The overall logo represents Pure Harmony.

  • Brand Typeface

    KALON's Secondary font is Barlow. The font is available free of charge. The Barlow font should be used in all printed materials that are editable. Barlow is available on most computers, as it is a system font. Making the web more beautiful, fast, and open through great typography.

    Brand Color

    Yellow is the primary brand colour for brand Kalon, for it signifies health, and consequently wellness. Muted hues that complement it were chosen for their appeal in international markets.

  • Visual style guide

    We had to develop a comprehensive brand identity system to support Kalon's brand personality. By choosing native symbols from India's classical wellness traditions, we crafted the brand’s visual signatures in contemporary styling. Our internal brief: manifest a dynamic brand that delivers outstanding nature’s goodness with the taste, texture and convenience of pop culture diet.

  • Packaging Design

    The challenge was to match the company's inventive portfolio of outstanding nutrition solutions with eye-catching, intuitive, contemporary design inspired by heritage.

  • Digital

    We wanted the brand’s digital presence to engage the new-age, nutrition conscious consumers right away. Content and interactive strategy focused on simplifying the complex topic of health and nutrition and delivering a lively, inviting experience.

  • BRAND CAMPAIGN

    We created striking visual assets that told an engaging story for the brand launch, with copy working hard to intrigue consumers with easily recognizable slice-of-life contexts. The aim was to make mind space for an iconoclastic, upstart fast nutrition brand. The hues, typography and composition were designed specifically for a vibrant social media campaign relevant to wellness.
