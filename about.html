About

As an art led graphic design studio, we are a comprehensive creative resource for branding, art & creative and space design projects. Our work across platforms and media is informed by art, science, consumer and cultural insights, and a uniquely classical foundation. Whether turnkey or component, we are focused on compelling consumer experiences. Be it branding, creative & art direction, digital or space – trust us to deliver the iconic.

Team

Rahul Gaikwad

Creative Direction

Rahul has had a varied design career of 20 years in animation, visual & graphic design and space branding at elite ad agencies and brand design firms, with prestigious awards and wins in his roster. Today he leads Tattva Design in in the field of visual design and branded storytelling.

He is responsible for creative concept and design direction for all branding projects for clients. He guides design, development and content to cohere and manifest a brand’s essence effectively for its consumers.

Prashanth Chinnamantur

Brand Content

Prashanth is a marketing MBA with twenty years of experience in marketing communications across media. With inter-disciplinary experiences across digital, media & entertainment, branding and advertising sectors, he serves Tattva's clients in telling impactful brand stories to their chosen audiences.

He works with the creative, design and development teams to conceptualize and create appealing brand story content over print, digital, film, broadcast etc.

Rajesh Sawant

Development

Rajesh has over 10 years’ experience in leading web development and UI/ Frontend programming across a range of projects involving brand and corporate sites, digital campaigns, social platform contests and webpages, and custom branded channels for online video content.

At Tattva, he works closely with the design & content teams to translate conceptual designs and brand stories into tangible web deliverables for clients. He manages the technology team that plans and executes digital experience initiatives across Tattva's proprietary and design service projects.

Girjesh Jaiswar

Design

An accomplished illustrator, Girjesh has delivered design and art projects for leading ad agencies and boutique design firms for the last 5 years. Awarded many times for his artful explorations of medium and theme, he has created and designed dynamic, aesthetically striking visual narratives for businesses and agencies.
