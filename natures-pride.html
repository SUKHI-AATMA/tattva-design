  • NATURE's PRIDE

    Nature's Pride, a packaged food products brand with a solid base in Maharashtra, reached out to us for developing a new brand identity. The goal was to help the brand achieve greater recognition in a burgeoning market space. The prime emphasis, they told us, was to better position its unique product portfolio to the new age consumers across India.

    We came on board with a strong, emotional brand story at the business's core, and reinvented the brand architecture with a unique characteristic playfulness and energy, and a lively, dynamic visual signature.

  • Brand Identity

    The new brand identity is a living, triumphant testimony of the farmer's toil. It reflects the classic farmer crowned with a turban made from nature's own abundance. It portrays his pride and happiness through the calligraphic expression.

  • Logo emblem

    We needed a strong visual icon that is adaptable to any surface, medium or platform. Something that is instantly recognizable as the brand's signature. The result is a minimalist, clean derivation from the brand's new visual storytelling framework, something that is simple as well as stand-out.

  • Brand Font

    Big Family Type Family. A low contrast script, slanted and bold characters. Bringing a hearty, casual and emotional flourish to the brand lettering, with swashes adding spice.

    Domine Type Face. Chosen for secondary content, it is friendly to the eyes, and easily consumable as long text on various mediums.

  • Communication & Brand Style Design

    Post identity revamp, we went on to recast Nature's Pride from a mildly inviting yet stoic presence, to a passionate and positive persona setting it apart on the market shelves. Today, the brand presents a trendy spirit with consummate appeal to the modern family audiences, across the country.

  • To the earth we return

    We think of ourselves not as a company, but as a farmers' collective, generating social and economic value for thousands of small cultivators in the regions we operate. We support the heroes of the land, the growers, by helping them with crop selection, sustainable farming, quality assurance and varietal improvement. We are humbly striving to creative a positive impact on the local agricultural populace, the very foundation of our success. In their welfare lie our gratitude, pride in our products and commitment to our employees and consumers.

  • Packaging Design

    We developed a packaging visual framework that blended the 'Mandala', a spiritual/ ritual geometric configuration common in Indian faiths and the symbols of nature's bounty. Each product has its unique Mandala filled with its actual ingredients, and so does each variant. This delivered a never-before-seen visual architecture for the point of sale, guaranteed to invite curiosity and engagement from shoppers. The aesthetic vibrantly portrays wholesomeness, as well as the dynamic, powerful relationships between food, humans and nature.

  • Website Design

    We were tasked to bring the brand's new identity to life in the digital space. We designed the site to deliver a slick, graphically sophisticated consumer interface, along with a lively, engaging voice telling the brand story. Naturally, the brand's digital presence was planned to offer a consistent and connected extension of the entire brand experience.
