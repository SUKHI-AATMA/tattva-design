  • THE CHALK CO.

    Sector : Fashion

    Services: Positioning, Messaging, Brand Identity, Design (UI/UX), Brand Communication, Product and packaging design.

    The Chalk Co. a new brand, launches an exquisite designer socks for men in the metros. We worked to find the pulse of the brand and build the entire personality, tone, voice of the brand.

    We came up with the simple brand identity which communicates the brand as a design centric brand. To stand out in the cluttered market we took an approach of designing the products with a story and make it more personalised as per the consumer personas.
